Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about renting with DriveEase

How do I book a vehicle?
Simply browse our vehicle selection, choose your dates, and complete the booking with secure payment. You'll receive instant confirmation.
What are the rental requirements?
You need to be at least 21 years old with a valid driver's license and credit card. Some vehicle categories may require you to be 25+.
Is insurance included?
Basic insurance is included in all rentals. Additional coverage options are available at checkout for extra peace of mind.
What is your cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 24 hours before your reservation starts. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a fee.
Can I modify my reservation?
Yes! You can modify your reservation dates or vehicle selection through your dashboard, subject to availability.
How does payment work?
We accept all major credit cards. Payment is processed securely through Stripe. You'll be charged when you complete your booking.
